I love what I do. Like truly, deeply love it. I get great satisfaction from being present for the moments when people begin to feel more like themselves. I sit in wonderment and admiration as people I work with they start to feel the richness and fullness of the life they were intended to have. It is truly an honor to witness a my client’s transformations towards joy, fulfillment and happiness. I am a Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW), and a Somatic Experiencing™ (SE) and Organic Intelligence™ (OI) Practitioner, as well as an adjunct teacher of the Organic Intelligence model, Human Empowerment and Resiliency Training (HEART™). I hold a Bachelor’s of Social Work from Warren Wilson College and a Master’s of Social Work from Columbia University in the city of New York. Following an 8-year career as a Social Worker at the Asheville VA Medical Center, working primarily with post-surgical patients and their families, with Veterans in the Mental Health Clinic and with homeless Veterans in need of housing across Western North Carolina, I opened a private practice near downtown Asheville. In my practice, I offer body-based psychotherapy, SE™-informed bodywork, professional and organizational trainings and therapeutic image consulting. I look forward to the chance to work with you!
